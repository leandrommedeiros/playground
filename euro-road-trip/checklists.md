### 🔧 Motorcycle Preparation
- [ ] Inspect tire pressures (front/rear calibrated for luggage load)
- [ ] Measure remaining tire tread depth (expecting ~5,000 km wear)
- [ ] Top up engine oil level to the maximum sight-glass mark
- [ ] Thoroughly clean, tension, and lubricate the drive chain
- [ ] Check brake pad material thickness front and back
- [ ] Cycle through all LED lights, signals, and hazards
- [ ] Scan engine casing and fork seals for hidden fluid leaks
- [ ] Adjust the electronic suspension preload settings for a full load
- [ ] Set custom riding modes (adjusting traction control for mountain rain shifts)

### 🔌 Electronics & Camera Gear
- [ ] Download complete offline navigation regions on phone and GPS
- [ ] Charge up all action camera batteries and clear storage cards
- [ ] Verify functionality of high-capacity portable power banks
- [ ] Pair helmet intercom headsets and update firmware versions
- [ ] Organize USB charging leads inside a waterproof bag
- [ ] Format all MicroSD storage cards to clean file structures

### 📄 Travel Documents
- [ ] Confirm passport expiration date covers trip duration
- [ ] Keep physical driver's license easily accessible
- [ ] Pack official motorcycle registration slip
- [ ] Verify international vehicle insurance green card validity
- [ ] Pack European Health Insurance Card (EHIC)
- [ ] Save digital/offline copies of all hotel reservations

### 🧳 Pack Lists & Tools
- [ ] Check condition of heavy-duty rain suit layers
- [ ] Pack thermal mid-layer clothing for high pass elevations
- [ ] Bring both warm-weather and heavy winter insulated gloves
- [ ] Pack a small tub of tire plug repair tools and Co2 canisters
- [ ] Pack a portable 12v electric mini-compressor pump
- [ ] Stash an all-in-one multi-tool and small container of chain lube spray
