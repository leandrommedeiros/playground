### 🎯 Goal
Transit efficiently across the Po Valley highway corridor to set up for the High Maritime Alps and French border.

### 🛣️ Highlights
- Fast Italian Autostrada cruising
- Piedmont rolling wine hill transitions around Asti
- Arrival at historic Cuneo at the foot of Maritime Alps

### 📸 Start recording
- Leaving Cortina mountain valley
- Transitioning into Piedmont hills
- Arriving at Cuneo town square

### ⛽ Suggested stops
- Autogrill coffee near Brescia (~200 km)
- Fuel & quick lunch near Piacenza (~320 km)
- Afternoon espresso near Asti (~470 km)

### 🍝 Lunch Stop (Approx. 12:30–13:30)
- **Location:** Piacenza / Parma highway corridor (Emilia-Romagna, Italy)
- **Restaurant Options:**
  - *Autogrill Fiorenzuola d'Arda* – The classic bridge Autogrill across the highway for rapid high-quality Italian paninis & espresso.
  - *Trattoria La Sosta* (Piacenza city bypass) – Quick authentic Emilia-Romagna pasta (tortelli d'erbetta).

### 🏨 Accommodation & Overnight Stay
- **Suggested Places to Consider:**
  1. *Hotel Palazzo Lovera* (Cuneo center - historic building with garage parking)
  2. *Best Western Hotel Cristal* (Modern amenities, private parking garage)
  3. *Hotel Fiamma* (Rider-friendly, easy access to alpine highway)
