### 🎯 Goal
Ride the best roads of the northern and southern Black Forest before tourist traffic builds up.

### 🛣️ Roads & Highlights
- B500 (Schwarzwaldhochstraße)
- Kinzigtal valley switchbacks
- B31 & L126 canyon curves
- Historic Freiburg city edge

### 📸 Start recording
✅ Entire early morning run of B500
✅ Forest sections around Wolfach
✅ Twisty descent into Hinterzarten

### 📸 Stop recording
- Urban traffic in Freiburg
- Lunch breaks & fuel stops

### 📍 Photo stops
- Mummelsee alpine lake lookout
- Triberg area forest viewpoints
- Hinterzarten mountain meadows

### ⛽ Suggested stops
- Morning coffee at Mummelsee (~30 km)
- Refuel in Wolfach (~110 km)
- Afternoon espresso in Freiburg (~230 km)

### 🍝 Lunch Stop (Approx. 12:30–13:30)
- **Location:** Hinterzarten or Titisee area (Black Forest, Germany)
- **Restaurant Options:**
  - *Café & Restaurant S'Suesse Winkel* (Hinterzarten) – Excellent Black Forest ham platters & authentic Black Forest cake.
  - *Gasthaus zum Ochsen* (Oberried) – Classic Baden regional dishes with outdoor seating.

### 🏨 Accommodation & Overnight Stay
- **Suggested Places to Consider:**
  1. *Hotel Restaurant Deutscher Kaiser* (Baden-Baden - base hotel night 2)
  2. *Der Kleine Prinz* (Baden-Baden - boutique stay with private parking)
  3. *Landhotel Untere Mühle* (Black Forest countryside alternative)
