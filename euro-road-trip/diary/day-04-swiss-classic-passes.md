### 🎯 Goal
Conquer the famous Swiss alpine loop featuring technical hairpins, high elevations, and historic cobblestone roads.

### 🛣️ Roads & Highlights
- Susten Pass (sweeping curves, tunnels & glacier views)
- Nufenen Pass (highest Swiss mountain pass road)
- Tremola Road (historic cobblestone switchbacks of St. Gotthard)

### 📸 Start recording
🔥 Susten Pass sweeping canyon sections
🔥 Tremola historic cobblestones (keep steady!)
🔥 Nufenen Pass descent into Val Bedretto

### 📍 Photo stops
- Susten Pass summit tunnel portal
- Nufenen Pass summit stone sign
- Tremola overlook point

### ⛽ Suggested stops
- Susten Pass summit café (~45 km)
- Airolo valley fuel stop (~110 km)

### 🍝 Lunch Stop (Approx. 12:30–13:30)
- **Location:** Airolo (Ticino, Switzerland)
- **Restaurant Options:**
  - *Ristorante Orologio* (Airolo) – Authentic Ticino risotto and polenta at the foot of Gotthard.
  - *Caseificio Dimostrativo del Gottardo* (Airolo) – Alpine cheese factory restaurant with local specialties.

### 🏨 Accommodation & Overnight Stay
- **Suggested Places to Consider:**
  1. *Radisson Blu Hotel Reussen* (Andermatt - Base hotel night 2)
  2. *Hotel Monopol-Metropol* (Andermatt center)
  3. *Susten Lodge* (Innertkirchen alternative)
