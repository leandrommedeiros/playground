### 🎯 Goal
Cross into Switzerland, complete highway transit smoothly, and tackle high alpine pass summits by late afternoon.

### 🛣️ Highlights
- Swiss Vignette border crossing at Basel
- Lake Lucerne shoreline sweeps
- Grimsel Pass granite walls & reservoirs
- Furka Pass iconic western climb

### 📸 Start recording
- Crossing Swiss border
- Grimsel Pass climb from Innertkirchen
- Furka Pass switchbacks & Hotel Belvédère

### ⛽ Suggested stops
- Border station at Basel (Buy Swiss Vignette & fuel) (~160 km)
- Coffee near Lake Sarnen (~250 km)
- Summit of Grimsel Pass (~300 km)

### 🍝 Lunch Stop (Approx. 12:30–13:30)
- **Location:** Meiringen / Innertkirchen (Bernese Oberland, Switzerland)
- **Restaurant Options:**
  - *Hotel-Restaurant Uriroth* (Innertkirchen) – Right at the foot of Grimsel/Susten passes; perfect rider stop.
  - *Parkhotel du Sauvage* (Meiringen) – Traditional Swiss lunch in Sherlock Holmes' town.

### 🏨 Accommodation & Overnight Stay
- **Suggested Places to Consider:**
  1. *Radisson Blu Hotel Reussen* (Andermatt - underground secure heated garage)
  2. *Hotel Drei Könige & Post* (Historic central hotel, dedicated motorcycle storage)
  3. *Boutique Hotel Crown* (Central Andermatt, comfortable rooms & bike garage)
