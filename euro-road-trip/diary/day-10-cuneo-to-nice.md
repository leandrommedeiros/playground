### 🎯 Goal
Ride legendary Rally Monte Carlo mountain pass stages before dropping down to the French Riviera.

### 🛣️ Roads & Passes
- Col de Tende valley route
- Col de Turini (world-famous tight hairpins with retaining walls)
- Col de Braus (serpentine ribbon of asphalt)

### 📸 Start recording
🔥 Legendary Col de Turini hairpin climbs
🔥 Col de Braus tight switchback balconies
🔥 Entering Promenade des Anglais in Nice

### 📍 Photo stops
- Summit monument on Col de Turini
- Col de Braus hairpin view overlook

### ⛽ Suggested stops
- Italian border fuel stop (~40 km)
- Col de Turini summit café (~110 km)

### 🍝 Lunch Stop (Approx. 12:30–13:30)
- **Location:** Col de Turini Summit (Maritime Alps, France)
- **Restaurant Options:**
  - *Hôtel-Restaurant des Trois Vallées* (Col de Turini summit) – Iconic rallying shrine filled with motorsport memorabilia and serving hearty mountain lunches.
  - *Les Trois Chamois* (Col de Turini) – Great mountain terrace crepes and hot paninis.

### 🏨 Accommodation & Overnight Stay
- **Suggested Places to Consider:**
  1. *Hôtel Beau Rivage* (Nice center / Old Town - secure underground parking nearby)
  2. *Hôtel Aston La Scala* (Central Promenade access with valet/secure garage)
  3. *Ibis Styles Nice Centre Gare* (Modern, budget option with private garage)
