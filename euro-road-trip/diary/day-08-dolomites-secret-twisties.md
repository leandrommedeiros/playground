### 🎯 Goal
Ride the single best motorcycle pass in the region (Passo Giau) early, followed by high-altitude glacial reservoir roads.

### 🛣️ Passes & Highlights
- Passo Giau (steep, relentless technical curves with iconic summit peak)
- Passo Fedaia (dam wall run beneath Marmolada Glacier)
- Lake Alleghe lakeside roads

### 📸 Start recording
🔥 Entire Passo Giau climb from Cortina side
🔥 Riding across the dam at Passo Fedaia

### 📍 Photo stops
- Summit of Passo Giau (unrivaled 360-degree panorama)
- Lake Alleghe shoreline overlook

### ⛽ Suggested stops
- Passo Giau summit coffee (~25 km)
- Fuel stop in Caprile / Alleghe (~80 km)

### 🍝 Lunch Stop (Approx. 12:30–13:30)
- **Location:** Lake Alleghe / Moena (Belluno / Trentino, Italy)
- **Restaurant Options:**
  - *Ristorante Stua de Michil* (Alleghe) – Lakeside terrace with gourmet Italian regional dishes.
  - *Malga Ciapela* (Base of Marmolada) – Mountain hut serving handmade pasta, local cheeses & polenta.

### 🏨 Accommodation & Overnight Stay
- **Suggested Places to Consider:**
  1. *Hotel Des Alpes* (Cortina - Base hotel night 3)
  2. *Hotel Franceschi Park* (Cortina center)
  3. *Grand Hotel Savoia* (Cortina luxury option)
