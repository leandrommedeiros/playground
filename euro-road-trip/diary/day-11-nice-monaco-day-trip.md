### 🎯 Goal
A light-luggage cruise along the coastal cliff roads, exploring the Formula 1 track layout and medieval hilltop towns.

### 🛣️ Highlights
- Grande Corniche cliffside scenic road
- Monaco F1 Street Circuit layout (Tunnel, Casino Square, Hairpin)
- Medieval perched village of Èze

### 📸 Start recording
🏁 Crossing border into Monaco
🏁 The iconic roar through the Formula 1 Tunnel
🏁 Grand Hotel hairpin turn

### 📍 Photo stops
- Casino Square (parking next to supercars)
- Prince's Palace overlook of Monaco harbor
- Èze village cliffside lookout

### ⛽ Suggested stops
- Short fuel splash before entering Monaco (~15 km)

### 🍝 Lunch Stop (Approx. 12:30–13:30)
- **Location:** Monaco Port Hercule or Èze Village (Monaco / French Riviera)
- **Restaurant Options:**
  - *Stars 'N' Bars / Quai des Artistes* (Port Hercule, Monaco) – Waterfront dining overlooking luxury yachts.
  - *Le Nid d'Aigle* (Èze Village) – Perched cliffside bistro with sweeping Mediterranean views.

### 🏨 Accommodation & Overnight Stay
- **Suggested Places to Consider:**
  1. *Hôtel Beau Rivage* (Nice - Base hotel night 2)
  2. *Hôtel Le Grimaldi* (Nice center)
  3. *Mercure Nice Centre Notre Dame* (Roof terrace & garage)
