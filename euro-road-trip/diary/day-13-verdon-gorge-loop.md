### 🎯 Goal
Circle the lip of Europe's deepest canyon on narrow cliffside roads with massive vertical drops.

### 🛣️ Highlights
- Route des Crêtes (D23 loop road)
- Moustiers-Sainte-Marie (stunning cliffside star village)
- Pont du Galetas overlooking Lac de Sainte-Croix

### 📸 Start recording
🔥 Camera angled down into the 700m canyon drop
🔥 Tunnels carved through solid limestone walls
🔥 Crossing Galetas bridge into turquoise waters

### 📍 Photo stops
- Point Sublime viewing platform
- Belvédère de l'Escalès cliff edge
- Lake Sainte-Croix bridge overlook

### ⛽ Suggested stops
- La Palud-sur-Verdon fuel splash (~35 km)

### 🍝 Lunch Stop (Approx. 12:30–13:30)
- **Location:** Moustiers-Sainte-Marie (Alpes-de-Haute-Provence, France)
- **Restaurant Options:**
  - *Cascades Restaurant* (Moustiers-Sainte-Marie) – Outdoor dining next to mountain streams under cliff faces.
  - *La Treille Muscate* (Moustiers) – Provençal specialties (lavender honey dishes & fresh salads).

### 🏨 Accommodation & Overnight Stay
- **Suggested Places to Consider:**
  1. *Hôtel du Commerce* (Castellane - Base hotel night 2)
  2. *Hôtel Nouvel Hôtel du Levant* (Castellane central)
  3. *Auberge du Point Sublime* (La Palud alternative)
