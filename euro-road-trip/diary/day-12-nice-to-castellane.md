### 🎯 Goal
Ride the fast, open, and sweeping curves of France's legendary Route Napoléon (D6085).

### 🛣️ Roads & Highlights
- Grasse perfume hills
- Route Napoléon D6085 (sweeping fast curves and cliff canyon views)
- Castellane rock tower entry

### 📸 Start recording
✅ Sweeping high-speed bends near Escragnolles
✅ Rock portal entry into Castellane

### 📍 Photo stops
- Napoléon milestone markers along D6085
- Col de Valferrière overlook

### ⛽ Suggested stops
- Grasse espresso break (~35 km)
- Castellane town entry fuel (~155 km)

### 🍝 Lunch Stop (Approx. 12:30–13:30)
- **Location:** Escragnolles / Séranon (Route Napoléon, France)
- **Restaurant Options:**
  - *Auberge Napoléon* (Escragnolles) – Traditional Provençal roadside inn catering to motorbikers.
  - *Le Relais de Napoléon* (Séranon) – Fast bistro lunch on the main route.

### 🏨 Accommodation & Overnight Stay
- **Suggested Places to Consider:**
  1. *Hôtel du Commerce* (Central Castellane, private motorcycle garage)
  2. *Ma Petite Auberge* (Charming central hotel with courtyard parking)
  3. *Camping du Verdon Cabins / Lodge* (Rider lodge with motorcycle space)
