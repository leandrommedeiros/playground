### 🎯 Goal
Complete a high-distance northbound transit day across Eastern France to reach the Vosges mountains.

### 🛣️ Highlights
- Sisteron Citadel cliff gate pass
- Foothills of Chartreuse mountain chain
- Timber-framed medieval old town of Colmar

### 📸 Start recording
- Passing through Sisteron rock gate
- Arrival in timber-framed Colmar old town

### ⛽ Suggested stops
- Sisteron morning coffee (~85 km)
- Fuel & quick lunch near Grenoble (~230 km)
- Afternoon espresso near Besançon (~460 km)

### 🍝 Lunch Stop (Approx. 12:30–13:30)
- **Location:** Grenoble / Chambéry area bypass (Isère / Savoie, France)
- **Restaurant Options:**
  - *Aire de Grenoble-Lumbin* – High-speed motorway service area with quick French bistro meals.
  - *Brasserie Le Zinc* (Grenoble outer bypass) – Authentic French brasserie for steak frites.

### 🏨 Accommodation & Overnight Stay
- **Suggested Places to Consider:**
  1. *Hôtel Le Maréchal* (Colmar Little Venice district, private courtyard parking)
  2. *James Boutique Hôtel* (Modern central Colmar, secure private underground garage)
  3. *Ibis Styles Colmar Centre* (Rider-friendly, private garage)
