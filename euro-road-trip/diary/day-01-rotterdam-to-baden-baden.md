### 🎯 Goal
Reach the Black Forest comfortably while avoiding arriving after sunset.

### 🛣️ Highlights
- Fast German Autobahn sections
- Scenic Rhine Valley transition
- Evening walk and traditional dinner around Baden-Baden

### 📸 Start recording
- Leaving home / loaded bike
- Crossing into Germany (A3 / Autobahn 61)
- First unrestricted Autobahn stretch
- Arrival at Baden-Baden

### ⛽ Suggested stops
- Coffee near Eindhoven (~110 km)
- Fuel & quick break before Koblenz (~280 km)
- Final fuel splash before entering Baden-Baden (~530 km)

### 🍝 Lunch Stop (Approx. 12:30–13:30)
- **Location:** Koblenz / Boppard area along the Rhine River (Germany)
- **Restaurant Options:** 
  - *Gasthaus zum Mühltal* (Boppard) – Traditional German schnitzel with river terrace.
  - *Rhein-Restaurant Cafe Central* (Koblenz promenade) – Quick lunch right on the water.

### 🏨 Accommodation & Overnight Stay
- **Suggested Places to Consider:**
  1. *Hotel Restaurant Deutscher Kaiser* (Central, garage/courtyard parking for bikes)
  2. *Aqua Aurelia Suitenhotel* (Next to Caracalla Therme, secure underground garage)
  3. *Hotel Merkur* (Rider-friendly, central location with private parking)
