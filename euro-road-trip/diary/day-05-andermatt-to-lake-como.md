### 🎯 Goal
Transition smoothly from snowy high alpine peaks down into the warm, palm-lined shorees of Italian lakes.

### 🛣️ Highlights
- Oberalp Pass lighthouse view
- Splügen Pass ultra-narrow Italian switchbacks
- Lake Como coastal road runs

### 📸 Start recording
- Descending Splügen Pass into Italian territory
- First views of Lake Como water line
- Coastal lakeside tunnel sections

### ⛽ Suggested stops
- Oberalp Pass summit (~20 km)
- Splügen border village fuel (~95 km)

### 🍝 Lunch Stop (Approx. 12:30–13:30)
- **Location:** Chiavenna (Lombardy / Valchiavenna, Italy)
- **Restaurant Options:**
  - *Crotto Ombra* (Chiavenna) – Traditional regional "Crotto" dining carved into rock caves; fantastic bresaola & pizzoccheri.
  - *Ristorante Mastai* (Chiavenna) – High-quality Italian pasta and local Lombardy wines.

### 🏨 Accommodation & Overnight Stay
- **Suggested Places to Consider:**
  1. *Grand Hotel Menaggio* (Lakeside location, secure private garage)
  2. *Hotel Du Lac Varenna* (Across lake via short ferry, charming lakeside views)
  3. *Hotel Bellagio* (Historic town center, private garage arrangements)
