### 🎯 Goal
Cross northern Italy and conquer the king of passes (Passo dello Stelvio) en route to the heart of the Dolomites.

### 🛣️ Highlights
- Valtellina valley roads
- Passo dello Stelvio (48 numbered hairpin wall climb)
- Val Venosta & entry into UNESCO Dolomites

### 📸 Start recording
🔥 Famous 48-hairpin wall climb up Stelvio
🔥 Descents into Trafoi valley
🔥 Cortina mountain skyline arrival

### ⛽ Suggested stops
- Fuel up in Bormio before climbing Stelvio (~130 km)
- Stelvio summit view stop (~150 km)
- Afternoon refreshment near Bolzano (~250 km)

### 🍝 Lunch Stop (Approx. 12:30–13:30)
- **Location:** Passo dello Stelvio Summit (Lombardy / South Tyrol border, Italy)
- **Restaurant Options:**
  - *Ristorante Tibet Hütte* (Stelvio Summit) – Famous panoramic deck right over the 48 hairpins; great bratwurst & polenta.
  - *Alpenhotel Capitani* (Stelvio Summit) – Warm indoor dining with traditional South Tyrolean dishes.

### 🏨 Accommodation & Overnight Stay
- **Suggested Places to Consider:**
  1. *Hotel Des Alpes* (Cortina - motorcycle garage, spa, quiet location)
  2. *Hotel Meublé Oasi* (Central Cortina, private parking, rider-friendly)
  3. *Hotel Pontechiesa* (Near town center, secure garage & bike wash area)
