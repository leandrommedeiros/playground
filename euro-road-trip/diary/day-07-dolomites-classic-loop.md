### 🎯 Goal
Complete the world-renowned Sella Ronda pass circuit, experiencing continuous twists against towering vertical rock walls.

### 🛣️ Passes & Roads
- Passo Pordoi (27 switchbacks on climb)
- Passo Sella (dramatic view directly beneath Sella towers)
- Passo Gardena (fast, smooth open alpine asphalt)
- Passo Falzarego

### 📸 Start recording
✅ Passo Pordoi ascent from Arabba
✅ Passo Sella wall sweeps
✅ Falzarego summit descent into Cortina

### 📍 Photo stops
- Fausto Coppi monument on Passo Pordoi
- Sella Towers viewpoint turnout
- Passo Gardena panoramic valley deck

### ⛽ Suggested stops
- Passo Pordoi summit coffee (~50 km)
- Canazei valley refuel (~90 km)

### 🍝 Lunch Stop (Approx. 12:30–13:30)
- **Location:** Corvara in Badia / Selva di Val Gardena (South Tyrol, Italy)
- **Restaurant Options:**
  - *Ristorante La Stüa del Gaiss* (Corvara) – Traditional Ladin mountain cuisine & wood-fired pizzas.
  - *L'Murin* (Corvara) – Bike-friendly brewery style restaurant with terrace mountain views.

### 🏨 Accommodation & Overnight Stay
- **Suggested Places to Consider:**
  1. *Hotel Des Alpes* (Cortina - Base hotel night 2)
  2. *Hotel Bellevue Suite & Spa* (Cortina central)
  3. *Miramonti Majestic Grand Hotel* (Cortina upper valley view)
