## 🏨 Evening Hotel Routine
- [ ] Top off the fuel tank if below half capacity (saves time next morning)
- [ ] Eyeball tire treads for embedded stones, wire, or sharp flint pieces
- [ ] Do a quick visual inspection of the bike frame and chain slack
- [ ] Plug in cameras, communication gear, and phones to charge station
- [ ] Move day footage onto a backup storage drive
- [ ] Check tomorrow's high-altitude mountain pass weather bulletins
- [ ] Pre-load and review tomorrow's GPS route tracks
- [ ] Hydrate thoroughly to fight elevation and wind fatigue
- [ ] Take 5 minutes to stretch out shoulders, lower back, and hips

---

## 🌅 Morning Departure Ritual
- [ ] Eat a hearty breakfast and drink plenty of fluids
- [ ] Fill up onboard water bottles/hydration packs
- [ ] Double-check active weather radar for mountain pass closures
- [ ] Pack panniers and top cases using the identical system every day
- [ ] Tug test all luggage mounting straps and latch mechanisms
- [ ] Spray and wipe down helmet visor clean of bugs
- [ ] Check that camera lenses are clear and ready to shoot
- [ ] Fire up navigation maps and ensure GPS tracking is live
- [ ] Verify phone is drawing power from the onboard USB port
- [ ] Secure earplugs tightly in ear canals
- [ ] Pull on riding gloves, secure helmet chin strap snug
- [ ] Take one deep breath...
- [ ] **Clutch in, click into first gear, and enjoy the open road!** ❤️
