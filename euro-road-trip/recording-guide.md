### 🔴 Always Record
- Mountain pass climbs and technical crest sequences
- Tunnel exits opening up into wide valley panoramas
- Distinct border crossings and geographical sign changes
- Sunrise crisp roll-outs & sunset arrivals at accommodations

### ❌ Stop Recording
- Monotonous multi-lane highway connecting links
- Stop-and-go downtown commuter city traffic
- Gas stations and routine maintenance stops

### 🎬 Great Cinematic B-Roll Ideas
- Morning routine of strapping down dry-bags on the V4S
- Startup animations on the Ducati dashboard screen
- Placing the helmet down on the bike seat overlooking a huge drop-off
- Steaming espresso cups sitting right next to the front tire
- Close-ups of the tire treads picking up dust or shedding rainwater
